Curriculum Vitae









I am a research assistant at VinUni-Illinois Smart Health Center (VISHC), VinUniversity, Vietnam, under the supervision of Dr. Huy-Hieu Pham, Professor Duy-Dung Le from VinUniversity, and Professor Minh Do from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Before that, I worked as a post-baccalaureate research assistant at University of Information Technology (UIT), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM) under the supervision and guidance of Professor Duc-Lung Vu from July, 2019 to December, 2021. I obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Engineering at UIT, VNU-HCM in December 2020.


I have a deep interest in Few-shot Learning, Optimization, and Transformer/Attention mechanism on Deep Learning models to tackle Computer Vision problems. I am looking for a Ph.D. position in Computer Science starting Fall 2023.
